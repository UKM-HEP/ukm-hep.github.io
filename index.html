High Energy Physics Data Analysis Starter Pack

The go-to webpage containing useful repositories, documentations, and supplementary materials for getting started on using High Energy Physics Opendata in data analysis. The end goal is to nurture and encourge young telanted Malaysian STEM students to take part in frionter scientific research, ultimately developing experimental particle physics capability in Malaysia. The webpage is authored by SiewYan Hoh (何守仁), he can be contacted by email: shoh@ukmNOSPAM.edu.my ; or siew.yan.hoh@cernNOSPAM.ch . The website is built from Skeleton@MIT.
